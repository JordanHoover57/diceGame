Welcome to the Game

  • Each Player will take turns clicking 'roll dice' to accrue points.
  • If a Player chooses to hold; their current score will be added to their global score
  • The Player's turn will also be over
  • If the current player rolls a 1 their current score is set to zero and they forfeit their turn
  • If the current player rolls (2) 6's in a row their 'Global Score' is set to zeor and they forfeit their turn
  • You can set what score you would like to play too!
  • If you don't set a score you will play to 50
